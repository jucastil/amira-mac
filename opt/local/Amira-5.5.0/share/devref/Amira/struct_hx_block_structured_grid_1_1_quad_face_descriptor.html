Initializes the object with given quad.

Faces are numbered between 0 and 5. (pIdxFace/2) determines the constant index dimension, (pIdxFace2) determines wether the minimum (=0) or maximum side is chosen. (pIdxFace+1)3) is the fast varying index.

Parameters:
dims Dimensions of the zone.
pIdxZone Index of the zone in the block structured grid.
pIdxFace face number within the zone.
i First (fast varying) index of the quad within the face.
j Second index of the quad within the face.
