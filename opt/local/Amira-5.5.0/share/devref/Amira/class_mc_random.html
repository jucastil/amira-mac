static double McRandom::randNormalDistribution (  )  [static]

Generates standard normally distributed random numbers by using rand() and applying polar Box-Muller transform.

Distributions with other mean mu and variance sigma^2 can be obtained by the transformation mu+sigma*randNormalDistribution().
