Compute Hausdorff distance from vertexSet to surface.

Trafos of vertexSet/surface are taken into consideration. The vector displacements are computed in the (non-transformed) coordinate frame of surface1. Array pointImages may contain the information about the nearest points on surface2 for all points of surface1. Return value is the number of successfully evaluated points.
