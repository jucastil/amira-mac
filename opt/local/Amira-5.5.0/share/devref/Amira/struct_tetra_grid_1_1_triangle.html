unsigned short TetraGrid::Triangle::boundaryConditionId

This denotes the type of numerical boundary condition.

Note, that boundary conditions may also be defined for inner triangles which are not part of a domain boundary. Likewise, there may be boundary triangles with no special numerical boundary condition. That is, why we need two variables.
