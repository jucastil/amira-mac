As an example, we provide a confocal image volume showing four chloroplasts in a spore of the moss Polytrichum Commune together with the PSF. The PSF is the image of a single point source, or as a close approximation, the image of a single fluorescing sub-resolution sphere. This example with more detailed information is available also in the first deconvolution tutorial.

The properties of the data set are as follows: Numerical aperture 1.4, Wavelength of the emitted light 0.58 micrometers, Refractive index 1.516 (oil).
