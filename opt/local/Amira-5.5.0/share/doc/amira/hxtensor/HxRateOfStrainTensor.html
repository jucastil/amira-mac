RateOfStrainTensor

  • Module
  • Mesh Option

Description

This module computes the rate of strain tensor for a given displacement vector field. If the displacement vector field describes the displacement of matter in space, the rate of strain tensor field will specify how the space is distorted by this field.

Analyze the resulting tensor field by extracting its eigenvalues (ExtractEigenvalues module) where the tensor field should be indefinite and thus may contain negative and positive eigenvalues for directions in which the matter is compressed or expanded during the distortion.

Connections

Data [required]

Vector field defined on a uniform grid (UniformVectorField3) and that describes the space distortion. The vector components must be floats.
